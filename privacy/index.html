Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026 · Effective date: set at launch

This is plain-language, not legal advice. It reflects the current build: a static site self-hosted on the r9 homelab, with Plausible analytics, no cookies, and no forms.

This is the personal site of Mario Seijo, founder of Minimo Studio. It's a digital business card, and I keep data collection to the absolute minimum. Here's exactly what happens.

The short version

  • No accounts, no sign-ups, no contact forms, no advertising, no cookies.
  • No tracking that identifies you personally.
  • The only data involved is (1) standard server logs and (2) anonymous, privacy-friendly visit stats.
  • Any link out (LinkedIn, Instagram, booking a call) takes you to a third party with its own policy.

What's collected

Server logs. Like nearly every website, the server that hosts this site automatically records basic technical requests — IP address, browser type, pages requested, and date/time. This is used only to operate and secure the site (diagnosing errors, preventing abuse). Logs are kept briefly and are not used to build a profile of you.

Anonymous analytics (Plausible). I use Plausible Analytics to understand traffic in aggregate — roughly how many people visit and which pages they view. Plausible is privacy-first: it uses no cookies, collects no personal data, and does not track you across websites or devices. The numbers are anonymous and aggregated.

Saving my contact. If you download my contact card (vCard) or scan the QR code, that happens on your own device — it doesn't send any information about you back to me.

What's NOT collected

  • No cookies of any kind.
  • No advertising or social-media tracking pixels.
  • No personal information — I never ask for your name, email, or anything else.

Links to other sites

This site links out to LinkedIn, Instagram, and a booking page (minimostudio.io). Once you follow those links you're on their platforms, governed by their privacy policies — not this one.

Your choices and rights

Because this site collects no personal information, there's very little to access, correct, or delete. If you're covered by privacy laws such as the EU/UK GDPR or California's CCPA and you have a question or request, email me and I'll help.

Children

This site isn't directed at children under 13, and I don't knowingly collect any information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, I'll update the date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@minimostudio.io.
